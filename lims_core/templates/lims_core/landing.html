At a glance

  • Sample registration and traceability
  • Workflow states with permission controls
  • Bulk status transitions with audit logging
  • Lab-scoped access using user roles

What this system is for

  • Maintain a single source of truth for laboratory sample identity and status.
  • Ensure consistent workflow transitions across staff and labs.
  • Support operational reporting and exception tracking.
  • Enable integrations with external systems through stable APIs.

Governance and audit posture

  • Role assignment controls what actions are allowed.
  • Workflow transitions are validated and recorded.
  • Bulk actions remain traceable and reviewable.
  • System health endpoints support uptime verification.
